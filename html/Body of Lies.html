About evaluation


Written by a journalist and set in some of the most sensitive places on earth, "Body of Lies" touches on themes of the war on terror and political friction. But in fact, the film is not politically sensitive, anyone with an eye can see that although the film has an extremely exaggerated appearance, it is still an ordinary original story about terrorism. And this political genre has a congenital disease: it's not entertaining. To compensate for this, and to better integrate the story itself into the political context, there is plenty of action and suspense
