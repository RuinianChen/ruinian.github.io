INTRODUCTION

About this movie


Django Unchained is a western action film from Weinstein International Pictures, directed by Quentin Tarantino and starring Jamie Foxx, Christopher Waltz, Leonardo DiCaprio, Samuel L. Jackson and Kerry Washington in the lead roles.


About plot


Django (Jamie Foxx) and his former masters however have a very brutal and cold history, but it is this experience that leads him to befriend Dr. Schulz (Christopher Waltz), a bounty hunter from Germany. Schulz is currently hunting down the Brittle brothers who have committed a number of murders, and only with Django's help can he successfully collect the hefty bounty, so Schulz promises Django that if he will assist him in catching the fugitives, dead or alive, he will set him free from slavery and gain his true freedom.


With Schultz successfully freeing Django, even though the two of them come from completely different backgrounds, including different personality habits, Schultz takes Django along with him, their target this time, a group of wanted criminals who are deeply guilty. Although following Schultz all the way to learn the art of capture and tracking, Django has actually never lost sight of his true purpose, which is to find and free his wife Brumhida (Kerry Washington), who were separated in a slave trade many years ago.


Even with different goals in mind, Schulz and Django eventually focus their search on the same person - that of Calvin Candie (Leonardo DiCaprio), owner of the Candy Farm, a notorious plantation where, simply for fun and amusement, slaves have to fight and kill each other fighting and killing each other. In the process of diving into the depths of an intricate mystery made up of all sorts of falsehoods and deceptions, the presence of Schultz as well as Django still inevitably arouses the suspicions of Stephen (Samuel L. Jackson), the most loyal slave butler around Candy, and secretly watches their every move, and the scariest part of this is that a very dangerous organisation is unmistakably surrounding them, and if If Schultz and Django want to escape with Brumshida, they will have to face a choice between fighting individually or as a team, or sacrifice for dignity.

MAIN ROLE INTRODUCTION

A black slave who was later rescued by bounty hunter Schultz and groomed to become an outstanding bounty hunter. Django is naturally intelligent and a fantastically fast and accurate marksman. Although free again, Django never forgets his lost wife and goes deep into the tiger's den of the most vicious slave owner, Calvin Candie, to stage a thrilling and bloody rescue.

A ferocious but charismatic plantation owner who liked to be called Monsieur in French, he was obsessed with black slave fighting, forcing his slaves to compete in jousts and enjoying the killings for the fun of it, the more he got out of it the more the black slaves tore each other apart and killed each other, ferocious but a gentleman of good manners.
