INTRODUCTION

About this movie


Inception is a film directed by Christopher Nolan and starring Leonardo DiCaprio and Marion Cotillard. The film is defined as "a contemporary action science fiction film that takes place within the structure of consciousness", with a plot that moves between dreams and reality. The film tells the story of Leonardo DiCaprio, a dream-maker who leads a team of agents, played by Joseph Gordon-Levitt and Ellen Page, to enter the dreams of others, steal secrets from their subconscious and recreate their dreams.


About reality


The plan for this film was born during the filming of Batman Begins 2: The Dark Knight, and Christopher Nolan had considered working with close co-star Christian Bale again, but Christian Bale, who had other filming plans of his own due to the filming of Terminator 2018 and the fact that Christian Bale had other filming plans of his own afterwards, suggested that Christopher Nolan look for other actors of his choice actor for the male lead role. That's when Christopher Nolan remembered another actor he liked but had never officially worked with, Leonardo DiCaprio.


Leonardo DiCaprio only knew that the film was written and directed by Christopher Nolan when he was confirmed to star in it, and even then he didn't know exactly what the subject matter was like.


Among the cast, five are Academy Award nominees: Leonardo DiCaprio, Tom Berenger, Pete Postlethwaite, Ellen Page and Ken Watanabe. There are also two Oscar winners, Michael Caine and Marion Cotillard.


When filming plans for this film were announced early on, it was rumoured that Leonardo DiCaprio's character's name was Jacob Hastley and Ellen Page played a character named Isabella, both later proved to be nothing more than official promotional smoke and mirrors when specific details of the film were officially announced, with Leonardo DiCaprio's character named Cobb and Ellen Page's character named Ariadne.

MAIN ROLE INTRODUCTION

Male No. 1, specialising in stealing the minds of others in their dreams, is the leader of this gang of dream thieves. This rare skill of Cobb's has made him a coveted target among corporate spies, and has cost him the people he loves and made him an international fugitive. Now, Cobb has a chance to redeem himself if he can only complete an impossible mission. Unlike previous missions, this time Cobb and his team are not tasked with stealing minds, but with implanting them.

Once the architect has built a particular level of the dream environment, it can only be given to the dream owner of that level, to avoid the possibility of the subconscious mind acting up if others become too familiar with the dream environment.
