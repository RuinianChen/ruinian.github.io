About this movie


Jim Carroll was one of the best players on his high school basketball team and basketball was an integral part of his life. When Jim dreams of becoming a basketball star, his best friend dies of leukemia, the high school team coach, teenage sex drive, coupled with the influence of heroin and other drugs, Jim begins to slide to the edge of degradation. Jim's mother tried to lead him on the straight and narrow, but he only went on the rampage. One homeless man changed his attitude towards life
