About this movie


The Wilderness Hunter is a 2015 drama film directed by Alejandro González Iñárrito and starring Leonardo DiCaprio, Tom Hardy, Will Poulter , Domhnall Gleeson and Paul Anderson.
The Wilderness Hunter" is based on Michael Punch's full-length novel of the same name, the story of a 19th century fur hunter Hugh Glass was wounded by a bear and other hunters robbed of his belongings and abandoned in the wilderness, the hunter experienced a painful miracle to survive and began to revenge the story.
The film won Best Motion Picture, Best Actor and Best Director in a Drama category at the 73rd Golden Globe Awards in January 2016, and won both Best Director, Best Actor and Best Cinematography at the 88th Academy Awards on February 29, 2016.


About plot


Hugh Glass (Leonardo DiCaprio) is a fur hunter, in a hunting trip by a bear beaten into serious injuries after being rescued by fellow boat captain Andrew Henry (Domhnall Gleeson), the captain hired two people John Fitzgerald (Tom Hardy) and Jim Bridger (Will Poulter) to take care of him. John Fitzgerald has no desire to care for Glass, and is so intent on keeping Glass's property for himself that he brutally murders Glass's son and convinces Jim Bridger to abandon Glass in the wilderness to die. The two originally thought that Glass would pass away in this way, but Glass with strong perseverance in the wild and savage land through the months, finally returned to safety and began a revenge plan.
