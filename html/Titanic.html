INTRODUCTION

About this movie


"Titanic" is an American 20th century Fox Film company, Paramount Pictures produced a romantic film, directed by James Cameron, Leonardo dicaprio, Kate Winslet starring.
The film is set against the background of the sinking of the Titanic cruise ship when it hit an iceberg on its maiden voyage in 1912. It tells a touching story of two people from different social backgrounds, jack, a poor painter, and Ruth, an aristocrat, who fall in love and abandon worldly prejudices. Jack gives up his chance of survival to Ruth.
The film was released on December 19, 1997 in the United States, On April 3, 1998 in The Chinese mainland, and on April 10, 2012 in 3D.


About reality


Titanic was the largest passenger ship in the world at that time, with the most luxurious internal facilities and the reputation of "unsinkable". Unfortunately, the Titanic was doomed on its maiden voyage from Southampton, England to New York. At about 23:40 on April 14, 1912, the Titanic collided with an iceberg, fracturing the starboard bow to midship and flooding five watertight compartments. At around 2:20 am on April 15, the hull of the Titanic broke in two and sank 3,700 metres to the bottom of the Atlantic Ocean. Of the 2,224 crew and passengers, 1,517 lost their lives, of which only 333 bodies have been recovered. The wreck of the Titanic, the deadliest peacetime maritime disaster, was not recovered until 1985 and was protected by the United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization.
