Year 2010 :

He took a break from promoting the release of the film Inception to accompany WWF experts on a field trip to Asia to see where tigers live in the wild and the threats they face. He met with people on the front lines of the international fight against trafficking.

In order to discuss how best to protect these endangered species. Dicaprio was one of the first Hollywood stars to donate $1 million to the Clinton Bush Haiti Fund, which was launched by Former PRESIDENTS Bill Clinton and George W. Bush after the Earthquake in Haiti.
